About the band

HEY TIGER is an acoustic duo bringing high-powered harmonies to crowd-pleasing covers. Their diverse song list spans multiple decades and genres – from paying tribute to renowned vocalists like Brandi Carlile, Adele and Cher, to reimagining rock legends like Ozzy Ozzborne, Linkin Park and the Red Hot Chili Peppers.
